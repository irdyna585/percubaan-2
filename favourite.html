FAVOURITE

Favourite Book

My favourite book is Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, which is the first book of the Harry Potter series. I like Harry’s character because even though he was still a kid, he acted bravely and matured beyond his age. The story is beautifully arranged, easy to follow/read and never feels boring even when reading long chapters.

Favourite Movie


One of my favourite movies is The Shadow's Edge. I enjoy watching it because the storyline is fast, full of mystery and has a lot of action scenes that keep me interested from the beginning until the end. The main character is truly iconic—calm, intelligent, quietly confident and always two steps ahead of everyone else. Overall, this movie is very worth watching because of its strong plot, memorable characters and suspenseful moments.

Favourite Anime


My favourite anime is Hunter x Hunter. I really like the combination of adventure, friendship and emotional storytelling. Characters such as Gon, Killua, Kurapika and Leorio each have strong personalities. The story arcs like Hunter Exam, Yorknew City and Chimera Ant are very memorable and beautifully animated.
